Developers - Destination line

We are a group of five students who study Information architecture at Malmö University. Our passion is about travel as much as is it to photograph and remember the highlights. The opportunity to share a fully journey from start in a professional way was not possible before we came up with our project idea. Our goal was to develop a travel guide made by people's different experiences and finally we have achieved our aim.

Destination Line is a social platform where you as a photographer can inspire and guiding people to their next journey. Our focus is to put the users travelstories visible to share with the world.

Create your own workspace to collect your lifetime experiences in different photo albums on your timeline. Get an overview of your own creation and share your gallery with others.

We believe that the feeling is as important as the content, therefore destination line is like a luxury magazine with qualitative pictures from all around the world present like an photo album with associated text.
